about Oceancare

OceanCare is a Swiss NGO committed to protecting marine life and habitats since 1989. It focuses on reducing ocean pollution, underwater noise, and plastic waste. OceanCare also works with international bodies to influence marine conservation policies.

About oceana

Oceana is the world's largest international advocacy organization solely focused on ocean conservation. Founded in 2001, it works to protect and restore marine ecosystems through science-based policy campaigns targeting overfishing, plastic pollution, and offshore drilling. Oceana has helped safeguard nearly 4.5 million square miles of ocean habitat globally.
